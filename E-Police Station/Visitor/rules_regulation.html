Important Rules & Regulations

  1. Always report emergencies to the police immediately.
  2. Do not interfere with ongoing investigations.
  3. Respect the privacy of victims and witnesses.
  4. Keep all your personal information updated with the local police station.
  5. Do not spread false information regarding any incidents.
  6. Be cooperative with law enforcement personnel at all times.
  7. Report suspicious activities to the police.
  8. Follow all directives given by police personnel during emergencies.
  9. Understand the laws in your area and comply with them.
  10. Use emergency contact numbers for urgent matters only.
