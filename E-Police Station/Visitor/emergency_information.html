Emergency Contact Numbers

  • Police Department: 911
  • Fire Department: 911
  • Ambulance Services: 911
  • Poison Control Center: 1-800-222-1222
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)

Important Emergency Procedures

  1. In case of emergency, dial the emergency contact number immediately.
  2. Provide clear and accurate information about the situation.
  3. Stay calm and follow instructions given by the emergency operator.
  4. If you are in a safe location, stay there until help arrives.
  5. Do not hang up until the operator has all the information needed.
  6. In case of fire, evacuate the area and do not attempt to extinguish large fires yourself.
  7. Always have a family emergency plan in place and know your nearest exits.
